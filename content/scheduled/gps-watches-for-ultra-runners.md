## What Features Make a GPS Watch Essential for Ultra Running?

Choosing the right GPS watch is crucial for any ultra runner. The primary features you should look for include long battery life, accurate GPS tracking, and endurance-specific data metrics. These elements are vital due to the extensive time spent on the trails, which can range from 6 to 30+ hours, depending on the race or personal training session.

### Long Battery Life
A GPS watch with a powerful battery is non-negotiable for ultra running. Devices like the Garmin Enduro 2, with up to 80 hours in GPS mode, ensure you can track your entire run without worrying about losing data.

### Accurate Tracking and Mapping
Precision in tracking your route using GPS is essential. Watches like the [Garmin Forerunner 965] (/tools/pace-calculator) provide top-notch satellite connectivity for accurate mapping, which can prevent you from getting lost on unfamiliar trails.

### Endurance Metrics
Data such as pace, heart rate, and elevation gain can be critical indicators of your performance. The ability to monitor these in real-time helps you make informed decisions during a race.

## Is Garmin or Coros Better for Ultra Running?

The debate over Garmin versus Coros for ultra running boils down to individual needs and preferences. Both brands offer excellent features and robust durability, but there are differences worth noting.

## How Important is Battery Life in an Ultra Running GPS Watch?

Battery life is arguably the most crucial feature for any ultra runner. When you're 20 miles into a 50K, the last thing you want is a dead watch. Some races and personal challenges can span more than a full day, thus, a long-lasting battery ensures you continue to accrue and monitor data.

### Leading Models with Superior Battery Life
- Garmin Enduro 2: Provides up to 80 hours of GPS tracking.
- Coros Vertix 2: Offers up to 140 hours in UltraMax mode, which is ideal for multi-day adventures.

Having a long-lasting battery will give you peace of mind during your runs and is particularly beneficial for those venturing into remote areas where charging might not be immediately possible.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a regular GPS watch for ultra running?  
Though a regular GPS watch may track general stats, it often lacks battery life and detailed endurance metrics required for ultra running. Opting for a dedicated ultra running watch is advisable.

Why does my GPS watch lose accuracy during runs?  
Environmental factors such as heavy tree cover or tall buildings can interfere with satellite connectivity, causing temporary inaccuracies in GPS tracking.

What's the best GPS watch feature for long races?  
Battery life is the most vital feature, as it ensures uninterrupted tracking. Long-distance lap data and easy-to-read screens also enhance race-day performance.

Do GPS watches help with training?  
Yes, they provide essential metrics like heart rate zones and pace, which can optimize your training regimen and improve overall performance.

How to prolong GPS watch battery life during a race?  
Utilizing battery-save modes and minimizing screen usage can extend battery life. Some models allow altering GPS accuracy to conserve energy.

Does Coros offer the same smartwatch features as Garmin?  
Coros focuses on sports-specific functionalities, with fewer general smartwatch features than Garmin. If you need a watch that blends fitness with lifestyle applications, Garmin might be a better option.
